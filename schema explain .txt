# 🛡️ RouteMate: Database Schema & Workflow Architecture

This document clarifies the 11 MongoDB collections in RouteMate and explains exactly what each is used for.

---

## 1. 📂 Core Models (Active Booking Flow)

### 👤 User (`models/User.js`)
- **Use Case**: The foundation of the platform's identity. 
- **Working**: Stores credentials, roles (`passenger`, `driver`, `admin`), and personal info. Every other document in the system references a `User` ID.

### 🚗 DriverProfile (`models/DriverProfile.js`)
- **Use Case**: Specific to the driver side of the marketplace.
- **Working**: Stores KYC documents (Aadhar, License), vehicle details (RC Book, verification status), and real-time availability/location.

### 🗺️ PublishedRide (`models/PublishedRide.js`)
- **Use Case**: The core carpooling/ride-sharing engine.
- **Working**: Allows drivers to post a "Future Route". It manages multiple seat bookings and ensures synchronization between drivers and interested passengers.

### 📍 Trip (`models/Trip.js`)
- **Use Case**: The archival record of every completed or cancelled movement.
- **Working**: Acts as the system's "History" ledger. While `PublishedRide` handles the booking, `Trip` stores the finalized details used for stats, revenue calculation, and historical logs.

### 🔔 Notification (`models/Notification.js`)
- **Use Case**: Communication and deep-linking.
- **Working**: Stores every alert sent to a user. Key fields like `link` determine where a user lands when they tap a notification (e.g., the Driver Request Details page).

### ⚙️ SystemConfig (`models/SystemConfig.js`)
- **Use Case**: Global administrative control.
- **Working**: Stores platform-wide constants like commission rates and global surge multipliers.

---

## 2. 🧩 Feature-Specific Models (Use Cases)

### 💰 FareConfig (`models/FareConfig.js`)
- **Use Case**: Per-vehicle pricing refinement.
- **Working**: Allows the system to have different base rates for different vehicle categories (e.g., ₹20 for Bike vs ₹50 for Sedan). It ensures that fare estimates are contextually accurate for the vehicle type requested.

### 🌟 Review (`models/Review.js`)
- **Use Case**: Trust and Safety.
- **Working**: Stores ratings and feedback between passengers and drivers after a ride. This data is used to calculate the "Average Rating" displayed on profiles.

### 🆘 SOS (`models/SOS.js`)
- **Use Case**: Emergency Response.
- **Working**: Tracks emergency triggers during a live ride. It records the location and timestamp of the alert, allowing admins or emergency contacts to be notified instantly.

### 💳 WalletTransaction (`models/WalletTransaction.js`)
- **Use Case**: Financial Accountability.
- **Working**: Records every credit/debit to a user's account balance. It is essential for tracking payouts to drivers and payments from passengers.

### 🏠 SavedPlace (`models/SavedPlace.js`)
- **Use Case**: User Convenience.
- **Working**: Stores recurring addresses (Home, Work, Gym) for passengers. It allows for "One-Tap" destination setting in the booking UI.
